UTSAV 2025 - Cultural Fest

Date: October 25 & 26, 2025

Time: 09:30 AM – 5:00 PM

Venue: Main Auditorium, Campus Ground

Organized by: Cultural & Arts Club

Overview

Utsav is the heart of our college’s cultural life – a two-day extravaganza where talent meets tradition and creativity flows freely. With inter-college competitions, workshops, exhibitions, and open mic sessions, it's a platform for everyone to showcase their artistic flair. Whether you sing, dance, paint, act, or organize — there's a place for you here.

Highlights
  • Group dance, Ramp walk, Solo singing & Drama competitions
  • Street plays & Flash mobs
  • Art & Photography exhibitions
  • DJ night & celebrity guest performance
