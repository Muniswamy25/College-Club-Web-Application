Food Fest 2025 – Flavours of India

Date: November 17, 2025

Time: 11:30 AM – 2:30 PM

Venue: Main Auditorium

Organized by: Culinary Club

Description:-

Get ready to tickle your taste buds! The Food Fest brings together a variety of regional dishes, international flavors, and student-run food stalls under one roof. From spicy street food to sweet delicacies, explore a diverse palette of cuisines while enjoying music, games, and food contests.

Highlights
  • Over 30 food stalls by students & local vendors
  • Food-eating contests & live cook-offs
  • Cultural dance + music around the food court
  • Eco-friendly & waste-free initiatives
